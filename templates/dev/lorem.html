What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text (sometimes referred to as dummy text or filler text) that has been used in the printing, typesetting, and graphic design industries since the 1500s. The text is derived from De finibus bonorum et malorum ("On the Ends of Good and Evil"), a philosophical work written by Marcus Tullius Cicero in 45 BC. The standard passage begins with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit," which roughly translates to "There is no one who loves pain itself, who seeks after it and wants to have it, simply because it is pain."

Unlike random text, Lorem Ipsum has a roughly normal distribution of letter frequencies, making it look similar to readable English. This is why designers and typographers have used it for over five centuries — it provides a realistic impression of how final text will look without distracting reviewers with meaningful content.

Why Do Designers Use Lorem Ipsum?

Placeholder text serves several critical purposes in the design process:

  • Focus on layout, not content. When reviewing a design, stakeholders often get distracted by reading actual text and start commenting on the copy rather than the visual layout. Lorem Ipsum prevents this by providing meaningless text that mimics the visual weight and rhythm of real content.
  • Simulate real content. Because Lorem Ipsum approximates the word lengths and sentence structures of natural language, it accurately shows how text will flow within a design — including line breaks, paragraph spacing, and typography choices.
  • Accelerate prototyping. Designers can quickly populate wireframes and mockups without waiting for final copy from clients or content writers, dramatically speeding up the design iteration cycle.
  • International compatibility. Lorem Ipsum is universally recognized in the design community, making it a shared language between designers, developers, and clients regardless of their native language.

Classic vs. Random Mode

Classic mode starts every generation with the traditional "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit" phrase, followed by additional randomly generated sentences. This is the most widely recognized form of placeholder text and is what most clients expect to see in wireframes.

Random mode generates entirely new text each time by shuffling the word dictionary. The words are the same Latin-derived vocabulary, but the sentence structure and word order are completely different each time. This is useful when you need multiple variations of placeholder text across different sections of a design, or when you want to avoid the instantly recognizable "Lorem ipsum" pattern.

Using Lorem Ipsum in Web Development

In modern web development, Lorem Ipsum is commonly used in:

  • HTML wireframes and mockups — Filling placeholder content areas to test layout before real content is available.
  • UI component libraries — Many design systems include Lorem Ipsum as the default text in button, card, and form components.
  • Content Management Systems — WordPress, Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD all use Lorem Ipsum in their default templates.
  • Typography testing — Evaluating how different fonts, sizes, line heights, and letter spacings look with realistic-length text.
  • Print design — Magazine layouts, book covers, and poster designs use placeholder text during the layout phase.

Alternatives to Lorem Ipsum

While Lorem Ipsum is the most traditional choice, modern alternatives exist for specific use cases: Cicero (different passages from the same source text), Pangrams (sentences that use every letter of the alphabet, like "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"), Hipster Ipsum (trendy, humorous placeholder text), and Bacon Ipsum (meat-themed filler text). However, Lorem Ipsum remains the most universally understood option for professional client work.
